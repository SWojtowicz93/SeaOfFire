Join a guild

A big part of Sea of Fire is being part of a community. Join a guild, make friends and fight together against other guilds.

Help your guild to the top, and prosper.

Collect "animals", train them and use them in fights.

We have many different animals roaming our different regions, catch them if you can. Use your animals in fights against other players, in events or friendly matches.

You can also trade or sell animals to other players.

Emerge yourself in the worlds history with story driven missions

A game is nothing without a good story. We have two. Chose to play as a Pirate or Dragon Handler. Both have vast story and a ever evolving missions.

Earn tokens and other rewards by succesfully completing missions
